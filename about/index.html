About Me

My name is Shanyan Guan. I am a direct doctorate student at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, supervised by Prof. Xiaokang Yang. During 2017-2019, I was working with Prof. Bingbing Ni. Currently (2019-), I am engaged in inverse graphics and domain generalization with Prof. Yunbo Wang. I received my B.S. in Electronic and Information Engineering in Xidian University in 2017.

My research focuses on human mesh reconstruction and related applications.
