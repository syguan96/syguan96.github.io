About Me

I am Shanyan Guan (官善琰), a researcher in computer vision and artificial intelligence. Currently working at vivo Imaging Department, I focus on AI Photography R&D. I received my Ph.D. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2024 under the supervision of Prof. Xiaokang Yang and Prof. Yunbo Wang, and my B.S. from Xidian University.

Industry Experience: I have interned at leading technology companies including Tencent, SenseTime, and miHoYo, working on computer vision and AI technologies across diverse domains such as gaming, AIGC, and mobile applications.

Research Focus: My research spans both academic and industrial applications. During my Ph.D., I focused on machine understanding of real-world dynamics and motion analysis. Currently, I concentrate on high-resolution image generation, video synthesis, and world models for mobile camera systems.
