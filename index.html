About

I am a researcher in computer vision and AI, currently working at vivo Imaging Department on AI Photography R&D.

I received my Ph.D. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2024 (advisors: Prof. Xiaokang Yang & Prof. Yunbo Wang), and my B.S. from Xidian University in 2017.

Previously, I interned at Tencent XR, SenseTime, Tencent YouTu Lab, and miHoYo.

Research: High-resolution image generation, video synthesis, and world models for mobile cameras.
